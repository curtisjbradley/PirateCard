Pirate Pass FAQs

How does the fundraiser work?

Businesses provide exclusive offers to MBHS supporters who have purchased a MBHS Pirate Pass Savings Card. When supporters show their Pirate Pass to a participating business, they can receive the advertised offer. A complete list of offers can be found on the back of the Pirate Pass.

Where can I use my card?

Our complete list of merchants can be found here.

Where do the funds go?

100% of the proceeds of our fundraisers go to support MBHS athletics, including all 23 sports teams.

How do I get a Pirate Pass?

Pirate Pass Savings Cards can be purchased at participating businesses. More information regarding price and locations will be available by June 2023 after Merchant Agreements are finalized.

Do Pirate Passes expire?

Yes, Pirate Pass Savings Cards are active from 8/1/2024 to 7/31/2025.

I am a business that would like to provide offers. How do I sign up?

You can sign up via our online form or by filling out a paper form.

How much does it cost to sign up as a new business?

Becoming a part of our fundraiser is entirely free. Fill out one of our paper forms or complete the form electronically at https://bit.ly/piratepassnew. You provide the offer, we do the rest.

Is there a deadline to sign up as a business?

Please complete the New Merchant Agreement by May 31st, 2024.

Who can I contact if I have any questions?

For any questions about the Pirate Pass Savings Card please contact Robert Dougherty (rdougherty@slcusd.org) or Curtis Bradley curtisbradley822@gmail.com)
